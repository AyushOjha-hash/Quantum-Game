⚛ Quantum TTT Rules

Superposition: Each move spans two cells simultaneously — like a quantum particle existing in multiple states.

Collapse: After 6 rounds, every move is measured and lands in exactly one of its two cells (50/50 probability).

Entanglement: Dotted lines connect moves that share a cell — their collapses are linked.

Strategy: Place moves that cover multiple winning lines. The collapse could go either way — maximize your chances!
