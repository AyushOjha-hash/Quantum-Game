HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click two cells per turn — your token exists in superposition across both.
  2. After 6 rounds, press Collapse to measure each move into one cell.
  3. Dotted links show entangled move pairs sharing cells.
  4. First to get 3 in a row after collapse wins the round.
  5. Each cell can hold at most 2 quantum tokens, and you may reuse the same pair of cells only twice per player to avoid spam.
